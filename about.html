About the Church

The Church of Infinite Knowledge is a community devoted to learning, teaching, preserving, sharing, and creating knowledge as the central practices of a life well lived. We ground everything we do in evidence, nonviolence, and the conviction that the freedom to seek understanding belongs to everyone.

Our story

The Church of Infinite Knowledge began as a quiet idea: that reverence, awe, devotion, study, and gathering flourish wherever people find something worth orienting a life around. For us, that something is knowledge itself.

We are an original community, built from the ground up on curiosity, evidence, and goodwill. We treat learning, teaching, preserving, sharing, and creating knowledge as the central practices of a life well lived, and we welcome everyone who feels the pull to understand the world more fully.

How we organize

Stewardship of the community is shared. Members participate as much or as little as suits them: reading, conversing, teaching one another, or simply being present.

Our gatherings happen primarily online, so that geography is never a barrier to belonging. Anyone, anywhere, may join.
